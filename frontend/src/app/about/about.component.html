About the Literacy Lab

At the Boise State Literacy Lab, we are committed to fostering literacy skills in children from grades K-12. Our program provides one-on-one or small group tutoring sessions led by undergraduate teacher candidates who are simultaneously completing coursework in literacy assessment and instruction.

Application Information

Enrollment for each semester (August and January) opens at the beginning of the semester and is limited. We strive to accommodate as many children as possible, but our capacity may vary depending on the availability of undergraduate teacher candidates.
